FAQ

How much does it cost to sign? ⌃
The price depends on the chosen plan. The more signatures included in the plan, the lower the cost per signature.
I sent the contract template to the manager, what next? ⌃
The manager will check the data and send the document for signing. You will receive a notification once it's done.
Can I work with the service through my CRM? ⌃
Yes. We support integrations with CRM systems via API and webhooks.
How often will funds be debited and what will be the payment amount? ⌃
It depends on your chosen billing cycle: monthly or annually. You can also track payment history in your dashboard.
